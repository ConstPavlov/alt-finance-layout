Click the "Registration" button in the upper-right corner of the site. Make a short registration and start earning income on your deposit!
How do I deposit my funds?
If you have cryptocurrency, you can deposit funds directly in that cryptocurrency. If you have pesos or U.S. dollars, you can deposit them directly from your bank card.
How and when can I withdraw my funds?
Just make a deposit and in the list of "Crypto strategies" choose the asset that you have, and the strategy that suits you.
How much can I earn?
At the moment, the strategy with the highest yields yields up to 7% per annum. Usually, this rate varies slightly over time, it may decrease slightly or become even higher! We are constantly working on developing the application and adding new strategies and assets!
How safe is it?
We only use the most reliable DeFi protocols, like AAVE which now uses over six and a half billion dollars of user funds in various cryptocurrencies.
